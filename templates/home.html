About Krishi AI

Krishi AI is born out of a profound respect for the Indian farmer. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional farming practices and modern technology, bringing predictive intelligence directly to the fields.

From predicting the perfect time to harvest, to instantly diagnosing crop diseases from a simple smartphone photo, Krishi AI is your digital companion in achieving sustainable and profitable agriculture.
